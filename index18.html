The story behind GummyChums

Why does taking care of your health
have to feel like taking medicine?

GummyChums was born from that one simple thought. Like many people, our founder was juggling multiple supplements a day — some for nutrition, some for energy, some for overall wellness. Remembering which pill to take, carrying different bottles everywhere, swallowing capsules every morning… it never felt enjoyable. It felt like another task on the to-do list.

So we asked: what if wellness could be as enjoyable as your favourite candy? “Chums” means friends — and that is exactly what these gummies are meant to be: the little friend you happily reach for every day. Not because you have to. Because you want to. Delicious, beautifully designed, science-backed — and made for real Indian lives.

Our mission is simple:
make wellness feel like a treat, not a task.
